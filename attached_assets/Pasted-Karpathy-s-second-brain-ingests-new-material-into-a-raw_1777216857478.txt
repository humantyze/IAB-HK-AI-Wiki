Karpathy’s second brain ingests new material into a “raw” folder, then has the LLM read it end‑to‑end, extract entities and concepts, and write or update corresponding markdown pages in the wiki layer. The model revises topic summaries, adds cross‑links, and notes contradictions so each source can touch many existing pages, gradually strengthening an evolving, interlinked knowledge graph instead of answering from scratch each time.[1][2][3][4][5][6]
